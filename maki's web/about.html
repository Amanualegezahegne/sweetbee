About us

Our Story

Sweet Bee Honey began as a small family operation with a simple mission: to share the pure, unfiltered sweetness of nature with our community. What started with just a few hives has grown into a trusted source of premium honey products.

Mission & Vision

We're dedicated to delivering natural, healthy honey while preserving the environment. We envision a world where sustainable beekeeping supports both bees and people.

Meet the Founder

Mahlet Berihun founded SweetBee Honey out of love for bees and a desire to provide natural products to her community.

Our Journey

  • 2021: Started with 3 hives in Mekele,Tigray
  • 2022: Expanded to 50 hives
  • 2023: Opened our first online shop
  • 2024: Partnered with local farmers to grow sustainably

Our Commitment to Sustainability

We never over-filter or heat our honey. We preserve enzymes, pollen, and nutrients. Our practices support pollinator health, local ecosystems, and the global bee population.
